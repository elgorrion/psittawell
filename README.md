# PsittaWell

A **non-commercial companion app** that turns a published companion-parrot welfare assessment into a friendly, offline-first phone app — a help tool for parrot owners and the scientists and vets who support them.

Based on:

> Piseddu A, van Zeeland Y, Hemsworth L & Rault J-L (2026). **PsittaWel: A welfare assessment tool for companion parrots.** *Animal Welfare* **35**, e34. DOI [10.1017/awf.2026.10089](https://doi.org/10.1017/awf.2026.10089). Original tool: <https://www.vetmeduni.ac.at/psittawel>

A KarmaNova Animal Technologies initiative. **Not monetised.** Not affiliated with or endorsed by the original authors unless and until they approve — see [`LICENSING.md`](LICENSING.md).

## Status

In preparation — application not yet built.

## What it does (planned)

- A guided digital version of the welfare assessment (75 questions across 8 sections), on a phone, working offline.
- Save a parrot's profile once, then reassess monthly and carry stable answers forward.
- An **end-of-assessment** overview that highlights areas worth looking into and flags anything that warrants an avian veterinarian or a certified parrot behaviour consultant — deliberately **no overall score and no diagnosis**, following the authors' methodology.
- **Private by default:** the data stays on the device.

## Principles

Faithful to the published instrument · private and offline · accessible and friendly · non-commercial · simple, not overbuilt.

## Platform

Built with Expo (React Native) for iOS and Android, with web as a bonus, using an on-device local-first datastore.
